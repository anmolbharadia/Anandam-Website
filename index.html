Welcome to Anandam Resort

Anandam Resort offers the perfect blend of elegance and functionality with state-of-the-art facilities spread over 86,000 sq. ft. Whether you're planning a relaxing getaway or a grand event, Anandam is designed to exceed your expectations.

  • Experience the grandeur of a three-star hotel with amenities designed for your comfort and convenience.
  • Enjoy a luxurious banquet hall spanning 8,100 sq. ft., ideal for weddings, conferences, and celebrations.
  • State-of-the-art air-conditioned conference halls with modern audiovisual equipment, perfect for corporate meetings.
  • Luxurious rooms and suites featuring premium facilities, including king-size beds, attached bathrooms, lounges, and more.
  • Dedicated outdoor spaces for open-air events, spanning over 3,500 sq. ft., alongside beautifully landscaped gardens.

Managed by the prestigious Shri Maheshwari Jan Kalyan Trust, Anandam Resort is committed to delivering top-notch services while offering a serene and welcoming ambiance. Make your stay truly memorable with us.
